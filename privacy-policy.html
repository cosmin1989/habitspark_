This Privacy Policy describes how HabitSpark ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the App on your Android device. HabitSpark is designed to be offline-first and private by default: the App does not require an account, does not ask you to log in, and does not upload your habit data anywhere.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit to our servers any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any account credentials (the App has no accounts).
  • Your habits, tasks, check-ins, streaks, mood logs, achievements, notes, or statistics.
  • Your optional in-app profile (display name, avatar, daily goal) — this is stored only on your device.
  • Your contacts, photos, files, location, or microphone data.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All App content you create — habits, tasks, check-ins, streaks, reminders, settings, your optional profile, and your premium/unlock status — is stored locally on your device in the App's private storage. This data:

  • never leaves your device through the App itself;
  • is deleted when you uninstall the App or clear the App's data in Android settings;
  • can be exported by you at any time from Settings → Export Data (the export file is created on your device and shared only where you choose to send it), and re-imported from a backup file via Settings → Import Backup.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own purposes — we never have it in the first place. The only third-party data processing in the App is the advertising and billing described below.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the App shows advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process certain data from your device, such as:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable identifier provided by Android);
  • IP address and coarse (approximate) location derived from it;
  • Device information (model, operating system version, screen size, language);
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, rewarded-ad completions).

This collection is performed by Google under its own privacy policy, not by us. Please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps

Where required by law (e.g., in the European Economic Area and the UK), the App displays a consent form provided by Google (User Messaging Platform) before showing personalized ads. You may choose non-personalized ads or withdraw consent at any time from Settings → Privacy Options inside the App. You can also reset or delete your Advertising ID in your device's Android settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

Purchasing the one-time Premium upgrade removes all advertising from the App, and with it the AdMob SDK's ad requests.

4. Purchases (Google Play Billing)

The App offers an optional one-time Premium upgrade processed entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details (card numbers, billing address, etc.); those are handled by Google under the Google Privacy Policy. The App only receives a confirmation that the purchase was made, and stores that confirmation locally on your device.

5. Notifications

If you enable habit reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. These are generated on-device; no notification data is sent to any server. You can disable them at any time in the App's settings or in Android's notification settings.

6. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad serving is configured to comply with Google Play's Families and AdMob content policies.

7. Data Security

Because your data is stored only on your device in the App's private storage area, it is protected by Android's application sandboxing. We recommend using device-level security (screen lock, encryption) to protect your data. Remember that uninstalling the App permanently deletes your local data — export a backup first if you want to keep it.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or hold your personal data on any server, requests for access, correction, or deletion of habit data can be fulfilled directly by you on your device (edit or delete data in the App, or clear the App's storage). For data collected by Google (advertising), please use the controls linked in Section 3 or contact Google directly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will be highlighted in the App's release notes.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@agiledev.pro.
